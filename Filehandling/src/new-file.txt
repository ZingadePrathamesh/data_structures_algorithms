विद्या नाम नरस्य रूपमधिकं प्रच्छन्नगुप्तं धनम्

विद्या भोगकारी यशः सुखकरी विद्या गुरुणां गुरुः।

विद्या बन्धुजनो विदेशगमने विद्या परा देवता

विद्या राजसु पूज्यते न हि धनं विद्या-विहीनः पशुः।।4।।
meaning: 
Knowledge is the beauty of man, that is a beauty. It is secretly Hidden wealth.
Vidya Gives us many pleasures. Vidya is the Guru of the gurus. If we ever go foreign, then The work come only.
Vidya Is the biggest Goddess. Vidya is Worshiped in kings, not wealth.
Therefore, the person who is without education is a animal.
